# Deployment Overview

Deployment requires both technical readiness and organisational readiness. An AI system that performs well in evaluation but is deployed without adequate governance, training, and monitoring infrastructure will fail to deliver its intended benefit.

## Prerequisites

| Prerequisite | Importance | Level |
|---|---|---|
| Completed evaluation phase (clinical utility demonstrated) | Necessary | — |
| Regulatory approval or clearance obtained | Necessary | — |
| Clinical integration design reviewed with IT and clinical leads | Necessary | — |
| Training programme prepared | Necessary | — |
| Governance and accountability structures agreed | Necessary | — |
