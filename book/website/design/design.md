# Guide for Design

The design phase is where responsible AI begins. The decisions made here — what problem to solve, whose needs to centre, what data to collect, what ethical and legal review is required — shape everything that follows. Mistakes at this stage are the most expensive to fix and the most consequential for patients.

The design phase maps to the following FUTURE-AI recommendations: G1 (engage interdisciplinary stakeholders), Us1 (intended use and user requirements), Un1 (clinical settings), F1 (sources of data heterogeneity), F2 (bias sources), E1 (explainability needs), G6 (application-specific ethical issues), G7 (social and societal issues), T1 (risk management process).
