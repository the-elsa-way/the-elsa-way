# Guide for Evaluation

Evaluation is the systematic assessment of whether an AI system does what it is supposed to do — safely, fairly, and for the patients it is intended to serve. It is not a single test at the end of development but a multi-dimensional process that covers technical performance, clinical utility, fairness, usability, robustness, and explainability.

FUTURE-AI recommendations mapped to this phase: Un3 (external datasets/multisite), F3 (evaluate fairness), G4 (adequate evaluation plan), Us3 (user experience and acceptance), Us4 (user experience and acceptance), Us5 (clinical utility and safety), R3 (robustness against real-world variations), E2 (explainability with end users), T2 (documentation including evaluations).
