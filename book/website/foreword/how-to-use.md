# How to Use This Resource

The ELSA Way is organised around the **AI development lifecycle**. You can enter at any phase — Design, Development, Evaluation, or Deployment — and find guidance relevant to where you are.

## If you are starting a new AI project

Start with the [Design](../design/design.md) section. Before writing any code, this phase covers the most important decisions: who will use the system, what problem it actually solves, what data you need, and what ethical and legal review is required.

## If you are mid-development

Jump to [Development](../development/development.md) for guidance on data management, privacy, fairness in training data, and human-AI interaction design. The cross-cutting [FUTURE-AI Principles](../future-ai/future-ai.md) guide provides quick access to all 30 recommendations by principle rather than by lifecycle phase.

## If you are preparing for clinical validation

The [Evaluation](../evaluation/evaluation.md) section covers how to plan a rigorous evaluation, what external and multi-site validation looks like, how to assess fairness across subgroups, and how to report your findings according to international standards (TRIPOD-AI, CLAIM, etc.).

## If you are deploying or operating an AI system

The [Deployment](../deployment/deployment.md) section covers integration into clinical workflows, quality control, monitoring, logging, regulatory compliance, and governance over time.

## If you are new to responsible AI

Start with the [Pathways](../pathways/pathways.md) section for a curated route through the resource tailored to your role.
