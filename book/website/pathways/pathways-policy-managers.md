## Key regulatory frameworks

- **EU AI Act** (2024) — classifies healthcare AI as high-risk; requires conformity assessment, transparency, human oversight
- **MDR/IVDR** — Medical Device Regulation / In Vitro Diagnostic Regulation; applicable to many AI diagnostic tools
- **GDPR** — General Data Protection Regulation; governs use of patient data
- **ISO 14971** — Risk management for medical devices
