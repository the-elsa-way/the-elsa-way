## Key reporting standards

- **TRIPOD-AI** — for reporting clinical prediction models using AI
- **CLAIM** — for reporting AI studies in medical imaging
- **CONSORT-AI** — for randomised trials involving AI interventions
- **DECIDE-AI** — for early-stage clinical evaluation of decision support AI

These are referenced throughout the Evaluation section.
