# Guide for Development

The development phase is where data is collected, models are trained, and the AI system takes shape. Many of the design-phase decisions get put to the test here, and new risks emerge that were not visible at the outset.

FUTURE-AI recommendations mapped to this phase: R2 (collect representative real-world data), F2 (collect data on individuals' attributes), G2 (privacy and security), G3 (implement measures against identified AI risks), Us2 (establish mechanisms for human-AI interactions).
