# How to Cite

If you use The ELSA Way in your work, please cite it as follows:

> The ELSA Way Community. *The ELSA Way: A Community Resource for Responsible AI in Healthcare*. ELSA AI Lab Northern Netherlands, 2026. Available at: [https://elsa-way.nl](https://elsa-way.nl)

For specific chapters or sections, include the section title and the date you accessed it (as the resource is continuously updated):

> The ELSA Way Community. "Fairness and Bias Assessment." In *The ELSA Way: A Community Resource for Responsible AI in Healthcare*. ELSA AI Lab Northern Netherlands, accessed [date]. Available at: [https://elsa-way.nl/evaluation/fairness-bias](https://elsa-way.nl/evaluation/fairness-bias)

## The FUTURE-AI framework

When referencing the underlying FUTURE-AI framework, please also cite the original paper:

> Lekadir K, Feragen A, Perianes AR, et al. FUTURE-AI: international consensus guideline for trustworthy and deployable artificial intelligence in healthcare. *BMJ* 2025;388:e081554. doi:10.1136/bmj-2024-081554
