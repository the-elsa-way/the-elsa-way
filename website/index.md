## Why The ELSA Way?

Despite rapid advances in AI for healthcare, most AI systems fail to reach clinical practice. The technical capability is often there; what is missing are the people, processes, and safeguards that real-world healthcare demands.

The ELSA Way is designed to close that gap. It is not a checklist or a policy document. It is an **integrated way of working** that embeds ethical, legal, and societal considerations at every phase, treating responsible AI as a design principle from the start.

It is grounded in the [FUTURE-AI framework](future-ai/future-ai.md) {cite}`lekadir2025futureai`: six principles for trustworthy healthcare AI (**Fairness, Universality, Traceability, Usability, Robustness, and Explainability**).

## Who is this for?

The ELSA Way is written for everyone involved in building and using AI in healthcare:

- **AI developers and data scientists** looking for practical guidance on responsible design and development
- **Healthcare professionals** who want to understand, evaluate, and confidently use AI tools in their practice
- **Researchers** publishing or validating AI systems in clinical settings
- **Policy makers and managers** establishing governance and procurement frameworks
- **Patients and communities** who want a voice in how AI affects their care

See the [Pathways](pathways/pathways.md) section to find the route most relevant to you.
